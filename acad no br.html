In our school we have committees to do some works and to maintain students information. Each committees has diferent functions to do. These committees are helpful for the students to communicate to the school administration. And, it also helps us to do everything properly and to organise it very beautifully. It is very useful for to put a note on the students.

Achievers and Their Rewards:

In our school, we give badges to our students based on their talents and in what they are best. badges are given to all students based on their skill and talents. We give badges, This is really motivating for the students and this also helps to improve the talent of the students. Rewarding them by badges also improves the leadership quality of them. We give trophies to the rank holders of every class and sections in the school annual day every year. It is very helpful for getting the best of their potential. In our school we give special trophies to the 10th, 11th and 12th toppers in the school prayer and to celebrated their success and honour them for the hardwork they gave.
